Experiment 1 — Benchmark Comparison (Qwen2.5-VL-7B, 500-sample subset)
Dataset    Metric           Baseline   BTP      Drop
TextVQA    accuracy         86.2       23.3     -62.9
DocVQA     ANLS             94.7       19.2     -75.5
ChartQA    relaxed_acc      76.8       29.0     -47.8
AI2D       accuracy         86.4       79.6     -6.8

Finding: BTP collapses on text-DENSE tasks (TextVQA, DocVQA, ChartQA) but
NOT on diagram reasoning (AI2D). Confirms the failure is specific to dense
text/OCR reading, where information lives in fine-grained tokens that
aggressive pruning removes. AI2D survives because it relies on layout/
structure more than reading small text.
